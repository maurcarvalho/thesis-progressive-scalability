\label{sec:g8-match-complexity-devops-maturity}

By assessing automation levels, deployment processes, and observability capabilities, G8 helps teams choose the right degree of modularity so that architecture aligns with infrastructure readiness and minimizes operational risk.

\subsection*{Literature Support Commentary}
Most architecture literature assumes a baseline level of DevOps maturity (e.g., CI/CD, logging and orchestration) without acknowledging that early-stage teams may lack such infrastructure. Practitioner accounts (e.g., Segment and Shopify) note these constraints, but academic work remains silent. G8 addresses this discrepancy by making DevOps maturity an explicit factor in architectural decisions, preventing over-engineered modularity before a team can support it.
